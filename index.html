Price Action

Price action trading analyzes security price movements through technical analysis, chart patterns, and market psychology to identify trading opportunities. Traders use patterns to enter/exit positions, manage risk through stop-loss orders, and anticipate market movements. Understanding market psychology is crucial for making informed trading decisions.

Master Tool Tredcode

Tredcode, developed by TCI, is a master tool that helps traders find the right trading opportunity by tracking big player activity and identifying the best stocks to trade. It includes a Nifty and BankNifty OI compass and a trend checker feature called Moneyflux. Traders can also learn from others' mistakes.

Live & Real-Time Accurate Signals

This platform offers live trade signals, access to the trading strategies of professional traders, their trading plans, and daily analysis. It also provides the best trading setups and allows traders to learn from the mistakes of others.

Swing Trading

Swing trading involves holding positions for days or weeks to capitalize on short-term market movements. Traders use technical analysis to identify trends and support/resistance levels. It's suitable for traders who don't want to constantly monitor the market and can be applied to various markets, including stocks, forex, and commodities.
